CSA Recreational League

The Charlotte Soccer Academy Recreational League has opened up for the Spring of 2023. The program is divded into 2 regions, North (Green) and South (Blue). The geographical breakdown can be viewed on the adjacent map. Both of these divisions will be open to players U10-U18, with paid coaching and organized practices. Rec teams will play amongst themselves throughout the 2 month season and participate in a comprehensive tournament at the end of the spring.
